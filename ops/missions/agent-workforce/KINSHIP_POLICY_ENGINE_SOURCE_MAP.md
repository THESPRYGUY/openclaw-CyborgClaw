## Implementation conclusion

This lane is grounded in already-ratified Kinship transport law and extends it in two tightly bounded ways:

- a named ACP-side policy engine becomes the transport admission authority
- an admitted, push-based inspector subscription makes that authority visible to operators without moving authority out of ACP
